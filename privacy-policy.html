Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 1, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Kitchen Match ("we", "our", or "the App") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our mobile game.

Kitchen Match is a casual puzzle game. We do not require users to create an account, provide a name, email address, phone number, or other direct personal information to play the game.

1. Information We Collect

We do not directly collect personal information such as your name, email address, address, or payment information. However, the App may collect limited information automatically through the App itself, Apple services, Unity, or third-party advertising services.

This may include:

  • Device identifiers, including advertising identifiers where permitted by your device settings
  • Advertising data
  • App usage and interaction data
  • Crash data and performance data
  • General device information, such as device type, operating system version, and language settings

2. Third-Party Advertising

Kitchen Match may use third-party advertising services, including Google AdMob, to display ads in the App.

These third-party services may collect and process information such as device identifiers, advertising identifiers, usage data, and other technical information to provide, limit, measure, and improve advertisements. Depending on your privacy choices and device settings, ads may be personalized or non-personalized.

3. App Tracking Transparency and Personalized Ads

On iOS devices, the App may request your permission to allow tracking through Apple’s App Tracking Transparency framework. If you allow tracking, third-party advertising partners may use your device advertising identifier to provide personalized ads and measure ad performance.

If you do not allow tracking, you may still see ads, but they may be less relevant and may be based on contextual information rather than tracking across apps or websites.

You can manage tracking permissions at any time in your device settings:

  • iOS Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking
  • iOS Settings > Privacy & Security > Apple Advertising

Information collected automatically may be used for the following purposes:

  • To display advertisements in the App
  • To measure and improve advertising performance
  • To maintain and improve App functionality

We do not sell your personal information. Data may be processed by third-party service providers, such as advertising and analytics providers, only for the purposes described in this Privacy Policy and subject to their own privacy policies.

Third-party advertising providers may process data independently according to their own terms and privacy policies. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party services, but we provide links above so you can review their policies.
